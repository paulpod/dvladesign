Tell us about taking a vehicle off the road (SORN)

When you need to make a SORN

You can make a SORN if you don’t use or keep your vehicle on a public road, eg you keep it in a garage, on a drive or on private land.

You must make a new SORN if you buy a vehicle and you want to keep it off the road.

You can’t transfer a SORN from a previous keeper.
