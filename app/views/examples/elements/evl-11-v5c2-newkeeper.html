Taxing a vehicle as a new keeper

Using a V5C/2 new keeper supplement (green slip)


Do not use this number if you have received a V11 reminder letter, or if you have a V5C in your name. It is intended for new keepers only.

You should have received your V5C/2 new keeper supplement (green slip) when you acquired the vehicle.
